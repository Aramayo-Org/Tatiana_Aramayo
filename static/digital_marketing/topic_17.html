Segment an Email List

Continuing from the previous activity, now that I've set the SMART goals for Feels Like Home's For All line, my next task was to segment the subscriber list.

Using subscriber responses to a series of questions about their experience, I segmented the list for three email topics:

  • A promotional email with discounts on space-saving furniture solutions for small kitchens
  • A promotional email with information about exclusive in-store sales
  • A newsletter with tips for keeping bedroom and living room furniture safe from pets
