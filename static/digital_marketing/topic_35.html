Set Seasonal Budgets and Bids

In this activity, I'm an e-commerce analyst for Personalized Presents, a business that sells personalized gifts, decorations, and apparel. Typically, Personalized Presents experiences a large increase in sales around the holidays in the USA (Thanksgiving through New Year's). The goal for this holiday season is to maximize profits, which is why a Performance Planner was used to calculate how much revenue was gained at different ad spend amounts.

My task is to use the data to determine the target ROAS and recommended budget for this period. To do this, I need to consider the drastic swings in revenue-per-click the business typically experiences during the holiday period. I analyzed a calendar of last year's revenue-per-click performance data and calculated how much to adjust the bids on certain days during the holiday. This way I've maximized profits while meeting the target ROAS.
