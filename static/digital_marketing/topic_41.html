Adjust Campaign KeyWords

Continuing from the previous activity with Good Mercury and the Sinclair Verde Lamp Company, the marketing team has decided to implement a Google Ads Search campaign. However, this campaign, which featured text that explains the company's mission of reducing waste, has not had much success yet after 30 days. In fact, over 90% of viewers are not clicking on the ads.

In this activity, my job is to adjust the specific topics and keywords associated with the ad campaign to increase the click rate. Doing so could increase clicks by as much as 20%.
