Analyze Social Media Performance

In this activity, I'm reviewing the data on two different Twitter posts from Great Grounds in order to determine which performed better. The Tweets were related to daylight savings time (when people set their clocks forward an hour), and the goal was to increase engagement.

Using the exported Twitter data on the spreadsheet, I've determined which Tweet performed better, analyzed why it was more successful, and proposed how to approach similar Tweets in the future.
