Analyze Data Using Pivot Tables

Continuing from the previous activity for L'Acier the online cookware retailer, now that I've pulled the data and used sorting and filtering to answer questions, my next task was to create three pivot tables to identify broader trends within the dataset.

With the pivot tables, I compared and examined the following KPIs over the course of a typical week:

  • Average number of sessions (site visits) at each hour of the day
  • Average number of conversions (account creations) at each hour of the day
  • Average conversion rate (account creation rate) at each hour of the day

After I finished creating and analyzing the pivot tables (found in the spreadsheet), I drafted an email to the rest of the marketing team to share my insights and recommendations of when to spend more money running the new ads and when to spend less.
