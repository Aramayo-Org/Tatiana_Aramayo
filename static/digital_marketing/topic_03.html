Create an Effective Response Search Ad

Per the Digital Marketing course, the company and scenario for this assignment:

"Oscar’s Bakery specializes in vegan, organic, and gluten-free baked goods. They have just launched a website feature that allows customers to order online. To promote this new feature, they are offering free delivery and 15% off on all online orders."

Oscar’s Bakery information:

  • Based in Boulder, Colorado
  • Founded in 1987
  • Offers healthy vegan, organic, and gluten-free baked goods
  • Open seven days a week
  • Uses local ingredients
  • Offers 25 different flavors of cupcakes
  • Provides the widest variety of baked goods in Boulder
  • Bakes custom cakes
  • Has won multiple awards

In this activity, my job was to create a responsive search ad for an ad group and organize it in a spreadsheet before entering relevant detail into the Google Ads tool. To improve my ad's performance, I crafted multiple headlines and descriptions that are relevant to keywords to match them to prospective customers' search queries. I also wrote ad extensions to provide additional important information.
