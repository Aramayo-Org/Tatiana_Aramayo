Write a Retention Email

Per the Google course assignment:

"Power Zone Fitness Clubs provide a wide variety of strength, cardio, and functional training equipment, as well as group fitness classes and personal training sessions. Power Zone is just about to launch their new app, Zone on the Go, which features online classes, progress tracking, virtual personal training, custom workout routines, and exclusive deals. To encourage existing club members to try the app, Power Zone is offering a free, three-month trial."

For this activity, I've been tasked with writing a retention email to announce the launch of the app. The target for this email is customers who have identified themselves as parents or caregivers. The goal of this campaign is to get at least 5% of these customers to download the app and sign up for the trial.
