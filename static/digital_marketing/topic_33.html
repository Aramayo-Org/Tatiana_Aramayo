Identify Brand Values

Continuing from the previous activity for EcoShop, the company has asked employees and managers to answer the following question about the Bath EcoShop brand: "If our brand were a person, how would you describe them?" Participant answers were compiled on a Google Jamboard, seen in the image below.

My task was to review the notes and find common themes that described the brand's values, and then brainstorm how the company can demonstrate those values to customers.
