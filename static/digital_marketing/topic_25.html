Make Campaign Budget Decisions

In this activity, I'm a marketing associate who completed work on a multichannel ad campaign for an e-commerce business. My task is to calculate the estimates of the campaign's return on investment (ROI) using return on ad spend (ROAS) and lifetime value (LTV) so that the results can be presented in a project debriefing.

Here I've used Google Sheets to make the calculations based on the campaign data and recorded my results and future budget decisions in the Google document.
