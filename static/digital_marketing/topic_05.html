Create Social Media Campaign Calendar

In this activity, I'm part of the social media marketing team of The Right Size, a size-inclusive clothing company whose mission is "to produce beautiful, affordable clothing that makes all people feel empowered, regardless of their size."

Here I began to build the May social media calendar on a spreadsheet, where I planned posts for the first week of the month. In it, I outlined posts around holidays, events, and company promotions happening that week. The document contains the provided research I used to plan the content for the week.
