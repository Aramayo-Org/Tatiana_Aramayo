Create SMART Goals for an Email Campaign

Per the Google course:

"Home, an upscale furniture retailer that does most of its business online. The company is launching a new line of furniture called For All, which is designed to expand their customer base. The For All line features the brand’s high level of craftsmanship, but at a lower price point than their previous offerings. Feels Like Home’s main business goal for the For All line is to grow their total active customer base by 30% within a year of launch."

In this activity, I'm in charge of the email campaign for the launch of this new line. The email marketing goals to support Feel Like Home's overall business goals are:

  • Grow the email subscriber list by the end of September
  • Increase the monthly email conversion rate

My task was to convert these goals into SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-Bound) goals using information from the meeting notes.
