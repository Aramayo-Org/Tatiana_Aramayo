Write an Apology Email

In this activity, my job is to write an apology email from Pixelated Games, a video game design company, for mistakes made in a game announcement email.

Previously, the marketing team had sent out a launch announcement for a new game, Infinity Gateway (the hotly anticipated fourth installment in a time-travel game series called The Thelos Chronicles). Unfortunately, that email contained two major mistakes, including a broken pre-order link and two different launch dates. To apologize, the company is offering customers a 15% discount on pre-orders for the next 24 hours.

Here I have written a follow-up email to apologize for the errors, send correct the link to customers, and offer a discount to affected customers.
